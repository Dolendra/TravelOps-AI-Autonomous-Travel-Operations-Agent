# TravelOps AI Support FAQ & Policies

Welcome to the intercity transit passenger support knowledge base. The policies detailed below outline guidelines on refund terms, seat upgrades, baggage allowances, and reservation rules.

---

## 1. Ticket Cancellation & Refund Policies

Passenger ticket refunds are calculated deterministically based on the time remaining before the bus scheduled departure:
* **Departure >= 24 hours**: 100% full refund of the ticket price paid.
* **Departure between 12 to 24 hours**: 75% refund of the ticket price paid.
* **Departure between 2 to 12 hours**: 50% refund of the ticket price paid.
* **Departure < 2 hours**: No refund is applicable (0% refund).

**Operator Disruptions & Cancellations:**
If a bus run is cancelled or disrupted by the operator (e.g. engine breakdown, highway safety issues), a **100% full refund** is automatically credited back to the passenger's card on file, regardless of time windows.

---

## 2. Loyalty Points & Seat Upgrades

Passengers can request upgrades on their booked transit runs subject to loyalty status:
* **Upgrade Eligibility**: A passenger must have a minimum of **1000 loyalty points** in their profile to qualify for an upgrade from their current seating class.
* If eligible, upgrades are processed automatically based on seat layouts.

---

## 3. Baggage Allowances

The following limits govern passenger baggage on all routes:
* **Check-in Baggage**: A maximum limit of **15 kg** per passenger is allowed in the lower cargo bays.
* **Cabin Baggage**: A maximum limit of **7 kg** of hand luggage is allowed in the passenger cabin.
* Excess baggage charges apply for weights exceeding these thresholds.

---

## 4. Reservation Holds & Payment

To secure a seat layout designation:
* **Hold Limit**: A seat hold is reserved in the inventory database for **5 minutes** under `HELD` status awaiting payment processing.
* **Payment Validation**: Credit cards are authenticated via the Luhn algorithm (Modulo 10 check). Invalid card numbers result in booking rejections.
